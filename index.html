How it works

Answer a few quick questions about what you’re seeing. PawsCheck helps you understand urgency and next steps, then gives practical “what to do now” guidance.

1) Describe symptoms

Tell us what’s going on. Add context like energy level, appetite, and timing.

2) Get a next-step recommendation

Monitor at home vs vet soon vs urgent care—presented clearly with what to watch for.

3) Take action with confidence

Use the “Find Emergency Vet” button and copy a vet summary for your appointment.
